Pixel Bot

🧨Pixel Bot is a Fun Bot that can make your server more interactive!
🎃The Bot has minigame commands, image commands, text commands and much more!

All the Commands:

🧩Minigame Commands:

❌!tictactoe <member you want to challenge> (you can just type !tictactoe to play against the bot)
🚢!battleship <member you want to challenge>
📃!rps <paper/rock/scissors>

📷Image Commands:

😸!image cute
🚗!image car
🍕!image food
🌄!image nature

💬Text Commands:

🔁!reverse <text>
😀!emojify <text>
